Highlighting

Kirby tries to autodetect the language of the paste, and highlight appropriately.

If you prefer to not have any highlighting, you can set the highlighting language to 'text', by appending "?l=text" at the end of a paste URL.

Alternatively, you can change highlighting for any paste by appending "?l=LANGUAGEHERE" to the URL. Below is a list of all support languages
